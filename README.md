## 📝 About

I'm Dark, a Creative Technologist living at the intersection of visual storytelling and digital craft.

My work spans three disciplines I believe are deeply connected — **photography** that finds quiet beauty in everyday moments, **video editing** that shapes raw footage into something that moves people, and **code** that builds experiences that feel natural and purposeful.

> *Good creative work isn't just aesthetic; it's intentional. Every frame, every cut, every line of code has a reason to exist.*
